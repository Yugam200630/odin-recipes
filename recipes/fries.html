Description

Crispy golden french fries are the ultimate side dish, made from fresh potatoes cut into thin strips and fried to perfection. Seasoned with salt and a hint of paprika, they are crunchy on the outside and fluffy on the inside. Whether served as a side with your burger or enjoyed on their own with ketchup, these homemade fries are far better than anything from a packet.

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Half teaspoon garlic powder
  • Half teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into thin even strips about 1cm thick.
  2. Soak the cut potatoes in cold water for 30 minutes to remove excess starch.
  3. Drain and pat the potatoes completely dry with a kitchen towel.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan to 160°C for the first fry.
  5. Fry the potatoes in batches for 5 minutes until soft but not browned, then remove and drain.
  6. Increase oil temperature to 190°C for the second fry.
  7. Fry the potatoes again for 2-3 minutes until golden and crispy.
  8. Remove and drain on a paper towel immediately.
  9. Season with salt, paprika, garlic powder, and black pepper while still hot.
  10. Serve immediately with ketchup on the side.
