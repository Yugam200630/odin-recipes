Lemonade

Home

Description

Fresh homemade lemonade is the perfect refreshing drink for a hot day, made with just three simple ingredients — fresh lemons, sugar, and water. Perfectly balanced between sweet and tangy, this classic drink is far superior to anything from a store and takes only minutes to prepare. Serve over ice for the ultimate refreshing experience.

Ingredients

  • 6 fresh lemons
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 cups cold water
  • 2 cups warm water for simple syrup
  • Ice cubes
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix 1 cup sugar with 2 cups warm water and stir until sugar completely dissolves to make simple syrup.
  2. Let the simple syrup cool to room temperature.
  3. Roll the lemons on a hard surface before cutting to get maximum juice.
  4. Cut and squeeze all 6 lemons and collect the juice in a bowl.
  5. Remove any seeds from the lemon juice.
  6. In a large pitcher combine the lemon juice, simple syrup, and 4 cups of cold water.
  7. Stir everything together and taste — adjust sugar or lemon juice as needed.
  8. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving.
  9. Serve over ice and garnish with fresh mint leaves if desired.
